About Us

We are a logistics company PT. Senopati Inti Artha was founded in 2024 with its head office in Jakarta. PT Senopati Inti Artha was founded by founders Abdul Rasyid and Diding Purwianto.
Armed with a logistics team that is experienced in its field, PT Senopati Inti Artha was established to be able to fulfill a wider distribution segmentation of consigned goods with the various services provided.
As client trust continues to increase, this company has begun to increase the number of its truck fleet and expand its delivery reach to various regions throughout Indonesia with competitive prices and the services we continue to provide optimally.
Apart from delivering goods in the Java - Bali region, PT Senopati Inti Artha can also distribute the relocation of household goods, work support tools for office areas, factories, warehouses and heavy equipment to various areas on the island of Kalimantan, Sulawesi, and Sumatra

Vision

To become a leading trucking company known for integrity, quality and innovation, by providing high-quality services that meet and exceed customer expectations. We are committed to being a reliable partner who prioritizes customer satisfaction and creates added value for all stakeholders.

Mission

Providing safe and efficient delivery services by maintaining high quality standards through human resource development, building long-term relationships with customers, utilizing technology for efficiency and sustainability, and optimizing profits ethically and professionally.
